# ShiftCare — Elevator Pitch

## One-Liner

**ShiftCare fills open healthcare shifts in hours, not days.**

---

## The Problem

Home health agencies lose thousands of dollars every week to unfilled shifts. A nurse calls out sick at 6 AM. A CNA no-shows. A patient needs coverage tomorrow. The agency scrambles — calling down a phone list, texting personal contacts, hoping someone picks up.

The current process is manual, slow, and unreliable. Staffing agencies charge 30-50% markups. Job boards are too slow for urgent needs. Group texts are chaotic and untrackable.

Meanwhile, qualified nurses and aides want more shifts but have no easy way to find available work near them.

---

## The Solution

ShiftCare is a shift fulfillment platform purpose-built for home healthcare.

**For providers:** Post a shift in 30 seconds. Qualified workers in your area see it instantly. When someone accepts, you get confirmation immediately — with guaranteed single-assignment (no double-booking, no confusion).

**For workers:** Browse open shifts, see the pay rate and location upfront, and accept with one tap. No applications, no waiting, no recruiter calls. If you want the shift, you take it.

---

## How It Works

1. **Provider posts a shift** — Role needed, date, time, location, pay rate
2. **Workers browse and accept** — First qualified worker to accept gets it
3. **Assignment is locked** — Server-side transaction prevents double-booking
4. **Shift gets filled** — Provider sees confirmation, worker shows up

---

## Why It's Different

- **Speed** — Shifts can be filled in minutes, not days
- **No middleman** — Direct connection between agencies and workers
- **No double-booking** — Database-level transaction safety guarantees one worker per shift
- **Workers are free** — Zero cost, zero friction for healthcare professionals
- **Simple monetization** — Providers pay a flat monthly fee based on volume, not per-placement markups

---

## Business Model

- **Workers**: Always free (maximize supply)
- **Providers**: Tiered subscription
  - **Free**: 3 shifts/month (try before you buy)
  - **Starter** ($49/mo): 25 shifts/month + worker contact
  - **Professional** ($149/mo): Unlimited shifts + priority features

Revenue scales with provider adoption. No per-transaction fees. No percentage cuts. Predictable SaaS pricing.
